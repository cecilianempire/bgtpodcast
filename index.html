Broke Girl Therapy

Broke Girl Therapy is a podcast hosted by Stefanie Maegan - a single girl fashionista looking for love in Los Angeles♥. The show highlights intimate conversations she has with her family, close friends, ex boyfriends, and failed dates. Stefanie talks about problems she encounters trying to find her match in a challenging dating scene filled with gay men and shady f*ckboys. The show sheds light on the struggles that hard working single women face in the modern dating world.
